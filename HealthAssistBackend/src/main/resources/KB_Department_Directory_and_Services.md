# Department Directory & Services
[KB-ID: KB-003]

## Cardiology (Tower A, Level 2)
Cardiology manages heart-related concerns from chest discomfort and palpitations to long-term conditions like heart failure. Typical outpatient services include **ECG**, **Echocardiography**, **Treadmill Stress Testing**, and **Heart Failure Clinic** consults. When users report **chest pain with shortness of breath** or **radiation to the jaw or arm**, the assistant should classify as **high risk** (per KB-004) and advise immediate emergency care rather than scheduling a routine appointment.  
**Contact:** Ext. 2201. **Hours:** 09:00–17:00 (Mon–Sat).

## Pulmonology (Tower B, Level 2)
Pulmonology cares for respiratory symptoms such as **persistent cough**, **wheezing**, and **exertional breathlessness**. Services include **spirometry**, **sleep studies**, and **asthma clinics**. Queries about mask use or nebulizer availability should be treated as informational; suspected **severe shortness of breath** requires emergency escalation.  
**Contact:** Ext. 2302. **Hours:** 09:00–17:00 (Mon–Sat).

## Orthopedics (Tower A, Level 3)
Orthopedics treats musculoskeletal pain, fractures, and sports injuries. The team provides **fracture clinics**, **post-operative follow-ups**, and **joint pain evaluation**. For **new trauma with deformity** or **open wounds**, the assistant should recommend urgent evaluation, possibly in Emergency, instead of routine OPD booking.  
**Contact:** Ext. 2401. **Hours:** 09:00–17:00 (Mon–Sat).

## Radiology (Tower B, Level 1)
Radiology offers **X-Ray**, **Ultrasound**, **CT**, and **MRI**. Some studies require **fasting** or **contrast**; instructions should be delivered only if verified in the Radiology SOP. For insurance coverage questions (e.g., MRI co-pay), integrate KB-005.  
**Contact:** Ext. 2105. **Hours:** 08:00–20:00 (Mon–Sat), limited service Sunday.

## Pediatrics (Tower A, Level 1)
Pediatrics supports child health, immunizations, and general pediatric medicine. One caregiver may remain **24/7** to provide comfort and support. The assistant should use empathetic language, avoid clinical judgment, and connect families to appropriate services swiftly.  
**Contact:** Ext. 2504. **Hours:** 09:00–17:00 (Mon–Sat).

## Insurance Desk & Billing (Tower A, Level 0)
Insurance queries, coverage checks, and billing adjustments are coordinated here. For **MRI coverage** or **pre-authorization**, the assistant retrieves policy guidance from KB-005 and can suggest a desk visit for final verification.  
**Contact:** Ext. 2600. **Hours:** 09:00–18:00 (Mon–Sat).
